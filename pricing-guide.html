Two Flexible Payment Options

We offer two pricing models to suit different business needs and budgets. Choose the model that works best for your fleet operation:

  • Monthly Subscription: Low upfront cost, predictable monthly payments
  • One-Time License: Pay once, own forever with optional maintenance

Monthly Subscription Model

The monthly subscription model provides access to our full fleet tracking platform with ongoing support, updates, and hosting included. This is ideal for businesses that prefer predictable monthly expenses without large upfront investments.

How It Works

  • Pay a monthly fee per vehicle
  • Cancel anytime with no long-term commitment
  • All software updates included automatically
  • Cloud hosting and data storage provided
  • Technical support included in all plans
  • Scale up or down as your fleet changes

Which Pricing Model Should You Choose?

Initial Cost Low - starts at $29/vehicle/month High - starts at $899/vehicle one-time
Long-Term Cost Higher over 3+ years Lower after breakeven point

Support Always included Included for 1-3 years, then optional
Flexibility Cancel anytime, scale easily Committed investment, but own forever

Breakeven Analysis Example:

Let's compare a 10-vehicle fleet choosing Professional tier:

  • Monthly Subscription: $24/vehicle × 10 vehicles = $240/month = $2,880/year
  • One-Time License: $1,199/vehicle × 10 vehicles = $11,990 one-time

Breakeven Point: $11,990 ÷ $2,880 = 4.16 years

Conclusion: If you plan to use the system for more than 4-5 years, the one-time license saves money. If you're unsure or might scale down, monthly subscription offers more flexibility.

Our Recommendations

Choose Monthly Subscription If:

  • You're a new or growing business
  • You have limited upfront capital
  • Your fleet size changes seasonally
  • You want predictable monthly expenses
  • You prefer expense vs. capital investment
  • You might scale down in the future

Choose One-Time License If:

  • You have stable, long-term fleet needs
  • You have capital budget available
  • You want to minimize long-term costs
  • You prefer ownership over subscription
  • You plan to use for 5+ years
  • You want control over updates/hosting

Choose Hybrid Model If:

  • You want ownership but need lower upfront cost
  • You want ongoing support included
  • You prefer not to manage hosting
  • You want flexibility to switch later
  • You're between the two options
