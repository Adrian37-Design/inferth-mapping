Start-up Cost: $0.00
Your Hardware Profit (Installation): You keep 100%

System Usage Fee (Paid to me): - $5.00 / month / vehicle
Your Net Monthly Profit: $10.00 / vehicle

My Role

Option 2: 60/40 Partnership (Revenue Share)

Total Revenue (Subscriptions): 100%

Your Benefit

  • We are fully aligned partners.
  • Great if you want shared responsibility.

Consideration

  • Requires a formal partnership agreement.
  • Your upside is capped at 60% of profits.
Option 3: Lease-to-Own

If your goal is to eventually own the source code yourself but you cannot pay upfront, we can structure a 24-month lease.

Monthly Technology Lease: $350.00 / month
Duration: 24 Months
Outcome: After 24 months, full code ownership transfers to you.

Your Benefit

Consideration
